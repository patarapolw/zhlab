# 十五个吊桶打水 – 七上八下

===

Shí wŭ ge diào tŏng dă shuǐ – qī shàng bā xià

Have one's heart clang like fifteen buckets in one well, seven going up and
eight going down – have one's heart pound with uncertainty, fear or turmoil
